# MiaoTI Raw Image Audit

Date: 2026-07-16

## Verdict

`../miaoti/archive/research/cat-meme-research/raw/` contains 13 ordinary cat
photographs, not the famous meme mother images promised by the surrounding
research notes. The files have no retained source URL, creator, license, or
download metadata. Several README descriptions do not match the actual image.

They may be used to study crop and pose preservation inside this local workspace.
They must not be treated as production sources or copied into public assets until
their provenance is recovered.

## What MiaoTI Got Right

The research notes did identify several useful archetypes: Smirking Cat, Happy
Cat, Crying Cat, Pop Cat, Keyboard Cat, Polite Cat, Nyan Cat, and Business Cat.
The failure was execution: those canonical sources were not actually collected,
verified, or connected to the generated outputs.

MiaoTarot therefore keeps the archetype research but discards the assumption that
the 13 local files are meme originals.
