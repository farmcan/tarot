# Famous Cat Meme Source Register

Date: 2026-07-16

## Product Rule

A source is meme-native only when a reviewer can name or recognize the meme from
the uncaptioned mother image. A funny pose is not enough. Production requires two
independent approvals:

1. cultural approval: canonical identity, origin trail, and strong recognition;
2. rights approval: commercial adaptation permission or an explicit license.

Until both pass, the source stays `research-only` and cannot enter
`site/public/`. Openly licensed ordinary cat photos remain legal fallbacks, not
substitutes for the meme layer.

## Selection Order

The first permission/outreach batch is Smudge, Happy Happy Happy Cat, Huh Cat,
Maxwell/Jess, and OIIA/Ethel. Together they cover disagreement, joy/action,
confusion, looping/defiance, and chaotic momentum. This is a stronger calibration
set than five ordinary poses because each image carries a pre-existing cultural
memory.

Keyboard Cat, Pop Cat, Bongo Cat, Nyan Cat, and Grumpy Cat are highly recognizable
but have more brand, edit-chain, or commercial-rights complexity. They remain
valuable product references but should not silently become shipped assets.
